Hi, I'm Vishwa.

I'm an Electronics and Communication Engineering student focused on cybersecurity, reverse engineering, Linux systems, and low-level understanding of how real-world systems actually work — and sometimes break. My interests currently revolve around offensive security, embedded systems, Android analysis, network behavior, and building tools that make technical workflows less painful.
