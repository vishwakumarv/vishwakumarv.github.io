I'm an Electronics and Communication Engineering student focused on cybersecurity, reverse engineering, Linux systems, and low-level understanding of how real-world systems actually work — and sometimes break.

My interests currently revolve around:

* Offensive Security

* Android Analysis
* Embedded Systems
* Malware Research
